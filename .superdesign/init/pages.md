# Pages - Bmbsqd Site

## Homepage (index.astro)
Single-page marketing site for Bombsquad - an elite technology consulting firm.

### Section Flow:
1. **Hero** - Full-viewport dark section with aurora animated background, large headline "Engineering Tomorrow's Digital Landscape", subtitle, two CTAs (Start Your Project + Our Expertise)
2. **Client Marquee** - Scrolling client logos/names on dark background (Sony Music, Paramount, Live Nation, Fortune 500, etc.)
3. **Global Presence** - Light background, 3 cards for LA/Stockholm/Singapore with color-coded icons
4. **Track Record** - Dark (#030303) bento grid with glass cards showing stats and testimonial
5. **Technology Expertise** - Light background, 3 cards for Web3/Blockchain, AI/ML, Enterprise Solutions
6. **Service Offerings** - Dark (#0a0a0a) bento grid with service categories + industry expertise grid
7. **Contact** - Light-to-muted gradient, 2-column layout with contact details and benefits
8. **Footer** - Dark (#030303) with logo, copyright, "Built for the extraordinary"

### Key Content:
- Company: Bombsquad (bmbsqd) - Premium tech consulting
- Tagline: "Engineering Tomorrow's Digital Landscape"
- Clients: Sony Music, Paramount, Live Nation, Fortune 500 companies
- Services: Tech Development, Advisory, Strategic Consulting, Implementation, Innovation Lab
- Offices: Los Angeles, Stockholm, Singapore
- Stats: $100M+ product exits, 25+ years combined experience, Fortune 500 client portfolio
